# The Catnip Artifact Incident
Teras Studio Jam 2026 Entry

A short, chaotic sci-fi action game where things go terribly wrong in a space station full of very loved cats.

🚀 Premise

You are a junior assistant aboard a research station in space. The work is important… but honestly, the real heart of the station is the cats. They’re everywhere. They’re adorable. Everyone loves them.

Unfortunately, a rival corporation decided to weaponize that.

They’ve deployed mutated catnip artifacts that irresistibly attract cats — and cause them to become aggressive whenever a human looks directly at the artifact.

Now the station is in complete chaos.

🧲 Gameplay
Explore the station
Locate dangerous catnip artifacts
Use your gravity tool to safely move them
Deliver them to the deactivation facility
Protect yourself… and the cats
🎮 Controls
WASD — Move
Mouse — Look around
Hold Left Mouse Button — Use gravity tool
🛠️ Tech
Engine: Unity
Style: Low-poly, stylized
Genre: Action / Comedy
📌 Notes

A short experience focused on physics interaction, light humor, and saving a station that really should’ve had better security… or fewer cats.
